Omaha Gives! is a 24-hour, online giving event organized by the Omaha Community Foundation to grow philanthropy in the metro area (Douglas, Sarpy, and Pottawattamie counties). The minimum donation is $10 and there is no maximum. Bonus dollars and prize money will be made available to amplify charitable donations. The goal is to inspire the community to come together for 24 hours to contribute as many charitable dollars as possible to support the work of local nonprofit organizations. Omaha Gives! is based on other successful giving days hosted by community foundations across the country.

Leading the way for Omaha Gives!, the Omaha Community Foundation has been helping individuals, families, and organizations facilitate their charitable giving since 1982. Over the years, those donors have given more than $1.2 billion to more than 3,000 charitable organizations, including educational institutions, human service groups, and agencies that help with arts, culture and humanities among others. To learn more about the Omaha Community Foundation, visit omahafoundation.org.

Omaha Gives! is an annual 24-hour charitable challenge to benefit metro-area nonprofits. This community give-together begins at midnight on May 20 with a minimum gift of only $10. We've got hourly drawings, prizes, and bonus dollars to boost your gifts. So gather your circles, schedule your gifts, and get ready to cheer for your favorite nonprofits throughout the day.
