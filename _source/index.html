Learn more about Omaha Gives!

Omaha Gives! is an annual 24-hour charitable challenge to benefit metro-area nonprofits. This community give-together begins at midnight on May 20 with a minimum gift of only $10. We've got hourly drawings, prizes, and bonus dollars to boost your gifts. So gather your circles, schedule your gifts, and get ready to cheer for your favorite nonprofits throughout the day.

Let's Give Together

The spirit of giving and a little friendly competition brings our community together in a very big way.

Nonprofits Win

Last year, more than 18,000 people raised $6.3 million for almost 600 area nonprofits in just one day!

Grow More Good

Nonprofits win bonus dollars for drumming up the most support. Hourly drawings and prizes only sweeten the give.

Give Two Ways

Schedule your gifts ahead of time or make donations throughout the day (Have more fun—give both ways!)
